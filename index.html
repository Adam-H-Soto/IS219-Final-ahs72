I'm studying Information Technology at NJIT (BS, Minor in Mobile & Web Development, expected May 2027) — Dean's Scholar, focused on making complex data legible. Not pretty for the sake of it, but actually useful in under ten seconds.

I treat engineering like engineering: every AI project starts with a spec — requirements, design doc, and task list approved before a single line of code. It keeps AI-assisted development intentional rather than improvisational. The specs for this portfolio live in /specs if you want to see the receipts. My work sits at the intersection of Python pipelines, LLM APIs, and the question "okay but would a normal person understand this?"
