I'm an Applied AI Engineering student with a passion for turning raw, complex data into tools that feel intuitive. My work sits at the intersection of data engineering, AI integration, and interactive visualization — building things that not only work, but communicate.

My current focus is on economic and public policy data: how do we take the sprawling complexity of cost-of-living, income, and opportunity across the United States and make it legible? The USA Standard of Living Explorer was my first answer to that question. EconLens is the next one — AI-native from the ground up.

I'm looking for a junior role at a company that cares about the quality of its data products. If you need someone who can wire a Python data pipeline to an AI API and ship it as something a non-technical stakeholder can actually use — let's talk.
